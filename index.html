Privacy & Compliance
HIPAA-Aligned Cephalometric Software — All Data Stays on Your Device

Architected from the ground up to align with American healthcare privacy standards. No patient data ever leaves your workstation.

HIPAA
HIPAA-Aligned Architecture
All PHI including patient names, dates of birth, X-ray images, and clinical notes are processed and stored exclusively in your local browser. No PHI is transmitted to external servers, eliminating the need for Business Associate Agreements (BAA).
ADA
ADA Standards for Patient Records
The American Dental Association guidelines require records to remain under the practitioner’s direct control. BCeph puts you in full possession of your records at all times — never passing through a third-party platform.
AAO
AAO Clinical Practice Alignment
BCeph generates structured, print-ready reports consistent with AAO documentation standards — skeletal classification, angular and linear measurements, and normative deviation assessments ready for the patient chart.
Local-First
No Cloud, No Breach Risk
BCeph has no servers to breach. Your data exists only on the device you control. For disaster recovery, export your full database as a .json file — restorable anytime without an online account.
How it works technically
BCeph is a single HTML file running entirely in your browser’s JavaScript engine. Data is stored via the localStorage API — sandboxed and never accessible to any external party. Reports open as local print windows. BCeph works identically with Wi-Fi off.

FAQ
Frequently Asked Questions — BCeph Cephalometric Tracing Software

Everything you need to know about BCeph before you start tracing.

Is BCeph free?+
Yes. BCeph is completely free with no hidden fees, subscriptions, or premium tiers.
Does BCeph store patient data on the cloud?+
No. All images, landmarks, and patient records stay in your browser’s local storage. Nothing is uploaded or transmitted.
Which cephalometric analyses does BCeph support?+
BCeph includes Steiner, Ricketts, McNamara, Downs, Tweed, Jarabak, and additional soft-tissue and airway modules — over 9 analyses total.
Is BCeph HIPAA compliant?+
BCeph is architected around HIPAA principles. Since no patient data ever leaves your device, there is no PHI transmission and no need for a Business Associate Agreement.
Do I need to install anything?+
No. BCeph runs entirely in your web browser as a single HTML file. No downloads, plugins, or accounts required.
